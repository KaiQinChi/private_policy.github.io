How does app use your Information?

The purposes include, but are not limited, to the following:

Google Map Services

Information:

  • Approximate location (based on network and Google map)
  • Precise location (based on GPS and network)

Usage:

  • Locate the events for you base on the address your enter
  • Caculate the time on the way from your position to an event

Voice Record

  • Record and play your voice for an event
  • Store your voice files

Phone Contacts

Information:

  • Contact name
  • Contact email
  • Contact phone number

Usage:

  • Select the phone contects as the attendees which invited to an event (Done by user)
  • Sent text message about event by user (Done by user)

In this app, there is no data collection and analysis base on a server, the data above is used locally by app .
